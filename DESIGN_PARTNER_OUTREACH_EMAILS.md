## DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

### Pre-Call Prep (15 min)

**Research:**
- [ ] Company website (products, AI/ML use cases)
- [ ] LinkedIn (decision makers, recent hires)
- [ ] News (funding, partnerships, compliance mentions)
- [ ] Tech stack (job postings, GitHub, blog posts)

**Goals:**
1. Understand pain points (compliance costs, security incidents, sales blockers)
2. Validate technical fit (AI/ML workloads, infrastructure)
3. Assess budget authority (can they commit to $44.5K?)
4. Gauge enthusiasm (1-10 scale, need ≥7/10)

---

### Call Agenda (30 min)

**1. Introductions (5 min)**
- Your background (credibility)
- Their role + responsibilities
- Company overview (products, customers, stage)

**2. Pain Discovery (10 min)**

*Questions:*
- "Walk me through your current AI governance process."
- "How much time does your team spend on compliance reviews each week?"
- "Have you had any security incidents or compliance violations in the past year?"
- "What's blocking you from closing enterprise deals right now?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand and solve one compliance problem, what would it be?"

*Listen for:*
- Manual processes (opportunity for automation)
- Time/cost burdens (quantify savings)
- Sales friction (revenue enablement angle)
- Recent incidents (urgency)

**3. Solution Fit (10 min)**

*Present:*
- "Based on what you've said, here's how PNKLN could help..."
- Demo (if applicable): JR Engine example, architecture diagram
- ROI calculation: "You're spending $X/year on manual compliance. We'd save you $Y."

*Questions:*
- "Does this address your top 3 pain points?"
- "What concerns do you have about implementing this?"
- "Who else needs to be involved in this decision?"

**4. Design Partner Specifics (3 min)**

*Explain:*
- 50% discount Year 1 ($89K → $44.5K)
- 2-3 hours/month commitment (3 feedback sessions)
- Co-marketing (case study, webinar)
- Timeline (Week 3 MVP, Week 9 production)

*Ask:*
- "On a scale of 1-10, how interested are you in being a design partner?"
- "What would make this a definite yes?"

**5. Next Steps (2 min)**

*If interested (≥7/10):*
- "Great! Next step is a technical deep dive with our engineering team. I'll send a calendar invite."
- "I'll also send the design partner agreement for your legal team to review."

*If lukewarm (4-6/10):*
- "What would need to change to make this an 8 or 9?"
- "Can I follow up in [timeframe] to see if the timing is better?"

*If not interested (<4/10):*
- "Totally understand. Can I ask what didn't resonate?"
- "Is there anyone else in your network who might be a better fit?"
