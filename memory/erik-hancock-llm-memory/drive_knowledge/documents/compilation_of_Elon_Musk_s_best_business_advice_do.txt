“Here’s a compilation of Elon Musk’s best business advice, drawn from his talks, interviews, and analyses by entrepreneurs and business observers:

Elon Musk’s Core Business Advice
1. Solve Meaningful Problems
Focus your business on tackling important, real-world challenges. Musk believes in working on ventures that have significant impact on society and create lasting change. His philosophy: “If something is important enough, even if the odds are against you, you should still do it.”aarongolub+1
2. Work Extremely Hard
Musk is known for his legendary work ethic, often working 80–100 hours per week. He says this dramatically improves your odds of success: “Work like hell…this improves the odds of success. If other people are putting in 40-hour workweeks and you're putting in 100-hour workweeks, then even if you're doing the same thing…you will achieve in four months what it takes them a year to achieve.”joorney+2
3. Embrace Risk and Accept Failure
Don’t fear failure—embrace it as part of innovation. Musk invested nearly all his money into Tesla and SpaceX at the risk of bankruptcy. He says: “Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough.”get2growth+1
4. Prioritize Product Quality
Devote yourself to creating the best product possible. Musk emphasizes that great products market themselves and are the foundation of great companies: “Great companies are built on great products.”bidsketch+2
5. Seek Out Critical Feedback
Actively solicit negative feedback, especially from close contacts. Musk argues: “Pay attention to negative feedback and solicit it, particularly from friends. Hardly anyone does that, and it’s incredibly helpful.”cnbc+2
6. Build and Retain the Best Team
Surround yourself with smart, motivated people who share your vision. Musk says, “A company is only as good as its people and how excited they are about creating.”reddit+2
7. Focus Resources and Stay Lean
Concentrate your resources on doing one thing exceptionally well—especially in the early stages. Avoid spreading yourself too thin.youtubeget2growth
8. Stay Close to the Front Line
Keep in touch with the front line of your business—customers, production, and employees. Musk is known for putting his desk on the factory floor and solving problems alongside his team.youtube
9. Be Persistent
Have grit, determination, and don’t give up unless you are absolutely forced to. “Persistence is very important. You should not give up unless you are forced to give up.”reddit+1
10. Innovate Constantly
Challenge the status quo and don’t blindly follow trends. Musk encourages continual innovation and self-improvement.get2growth+1
11. Optimize Meetings and Communication
Minimize meetings and bureaucracy. Solve problems immediately when possible rather than delegating or waiting for meetings.youtube

Notable Musk Quotes
“If you’re trying to create a company, it’s like baking a cake. You have to have all the ingredients in the right proportion.”reddit
“Take as much feedback from as many people as you can about whatever idea you have. Seek critical feedback. Ask them what’s wrong.”cnbc
“My biggest mistake is probably weighing too much on someone’s talent and not [enough on] someone’s personality. I think it matters [a lot] whether someone has a good heart.”reddit

For Immediate Inspiration
Start with one actionable idea: Rather than being overwhelmed, Musk suggests picking one thing you can do now to improve your business and act on it immediately.youtube

Here’s Elon Musk’s most actionable advice on hiring, launching and leading tech startups, and getting things done as a CEO, based on his most cited interviews, talks, and written reflections:

Elon Musk’s Advice on Hiring
Hire Smart, Driven, Innovative People
“A company is only as good as its people and how excited they are about creating.” At Tesla, SpaceX, and his other companies, Musk focuses on hiring people who are both highly skilled and passionate about the mission.aarongolub+1
Personality Matters
Musk admits his biggest mistake is “weighing too much on someone’s talent and not enough on someone’s personality.” He emphasizes looking for integrity and good character, not just technical prowess.aarongolub
Create an Inspiring Vision
Talented people are drawn to bold missions. Musk regularly communicates strong, world-changing visions to attract and retain top talent.instagram+1

Launching Startups & Tech Companies
Solve a Massive, Meaningful Problem
“If something is important enough, even if the odds are against you, you should still do it.” Focus on technology that has the potential for transformative impact.reddit+2
Build Products People Truly Love
“Build a product that your customers love.” Musk insists most startups fail because their product is merely “okay”—make it so good people root for you.startupbell
Make It 10x Better Than Competitors
Don’t settle for incremental improvement; aim for products that are radically better.startupbell+1
Be Frugal & Resourceful
Launch quickly, iterate rapidly, and avoid unnecessary expenses. Musk’s “first principles thinking” encourages designing solutions from the ground up, not copying competitors.youtube+1
Constantly Seek Feedback & Criticism
“A well thought out critique of whatever you’re doing is as valuable as gold.” Ask everyone, especially your friends, for honest feedback.youtubefinance.yahoo
Move Fast & Iterate
Don’t wait for the perfect moment or plan. “The first step is to establish that something is possible; then probability will occur.” Build, launch, and keep improving.instagram+1youtube

Leading Tech Startups & Getting Things Done as CEO
Obsess Over Product Quality
Musk personally gets involved in engineering and design, setting a standard for obsessing over every detail until the product is remarkable—this applies to cars, rockets, and software.aarongolub+1
Lead From the Front
Musk is known for being present on the factory floor and working side by side with his teams, especially in critical moments.aarongolub
Make Decisions Quickly
Minimize bureaucracy and meetings; solve things immediately when possible.youtube
Persist Through Setbacks
“Persistence is very important. You should not give up unless you are forced to give up.” Keep pushing despite adversity.get2growth+1
Request and Accept Constructive Criticism
Don’t just tolerate feedback—actively seek it to improve processes and products.finance.yahoo
Focus on the Core Mission
Always align activities and resources around the company’s most vital objectives and the primary product — don’t get distracted.instagram+1

Summary List: Key Things Musk Wants Founders and CEOs To Do
Hire for brilliance, drive, and integrity. Build a team aligned with your mission.
Launch startups by tackling huge problems, and build products people truly love—not just “like.”
Lead as CEO by being hands-on, obsessing over product quality, and seeking feedback relentlessly.
Move fast, keep improving, and minimize wasted time on meetings and bureaucracy.
Don’t give up, even in the face of major failures. Persistence matters more than perfection.instagram+4
