## 1. The Core Thesis: "Compliance-Wrapped Autonomy"
We are no longer just selling "AI Tools." We are selling **Liability Reduction**.
*   **The Problem:** LLMs hallucinate citations (Law) and drug interactions (Bio). Professionals cannot trust them.
*   **The Solution:** The **Omega Hybrid Loop**.
    1.  **GCA (Architect):** Reasons and drafts the content.

## 3. The Implementation: "Research-Grade Verification"
We replace "Github" with "Domain Databases" as the verification target.

**The Loop:**
1.  **GCA** writes a draft: "Based on *Roe v. Wade*..."
2.  **Jetski** triggers `verify_citation("Roe v. Wade")`.
3.  **Jetski** browses CourtListener/Westlaw.
    *   *If Found:* Downloads PDF, confirms quote.
    *   *If Hallucinated:* Flags error, forces GCA to rewrite.
4.  **Judge6** validates final output against "Bluebook" styling.
