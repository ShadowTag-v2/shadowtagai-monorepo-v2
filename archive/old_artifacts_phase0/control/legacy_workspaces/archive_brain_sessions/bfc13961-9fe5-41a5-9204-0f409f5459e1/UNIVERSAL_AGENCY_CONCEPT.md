# THE UNIVERSAL AGENCY: AI REPRESENTATION
*Vertical: ShadowTag / Professional Services*

## 1. The Core Concept
**"CAA for Everyone Else"**
A platform that provides elite, AI-driven "Agents" to professionals who need representation but are underserved by traditional human agencies (e.g., mid-tier Actors, College Coaches, Private Equity Managers, Freelance Executives).

**Logline**: Everyone deserves a shark in their corner. The Universal Agency provides a tireless, AI-powered representative to negotiate contracts, scout opportunities, and manage careers.

## 2. Target Markets ("Humans who need Agents")
1.  **Actors/Creatives**:
    *   *Problem*: Top 1% get CAA/WME. The rest get nothing.
    *   *Solution*: AI Agent scans casting calls, submits profiles, and negotiates standard union contracts.
2.  **College Coaches**:
    *   *Problem*: High turnover, complex contracts, limited network.
    *   *Solution*: AI Agent tracks coaching carousel, analyzes salary data, and negotiates buyouts.
3.  **Private Equity Managers**:
    *   *Problem*: Fundraising is a grind. Deal flow is noisy.
    *   *Solution*: "Capital Agent" finds LPs, matches investment thesis, and automates DD.

## 3. The "Agent" Tech Stack (Judge #6 Powered)
*   **Negotiation Engine**: Trained on thousands of contracts (Sportrac, SAG-AFTRA, SEC filings).
*   **Opportunity Scanner**: Scrapes fragmented job/deal boards (Instructional, breakdowns, etc.).
*   **Reputation Manager**: Monitors press/socials and advises on PR.
*   **Compliance**: `LawJudge` reviews every contract before signing.

## 4. Valuation Analysis
**Market Size**: Huge latent demand.
*   **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: $5B+ (Commission on negotiated deals).
*   **Monetization**:
    *   **Success Fee**: 5-10% (vs Standard 10-20%).
    *   **Subscription**: $500/mo for "Active Representation".

## 5. Strategic Advantage
*   **Scale**: Human agents manage ~50 clients max. AI Agents manage infinite.
*   **Data**: "We know what every coach is paid." (Asymmetric Information).
*   **Speed**: Instant contract review vs. "I'll call you next week."

## 6. Execution Roadmap
1.  **Phase 1 (The "Jerry Maguire" prototype)**: Focus on ONE niche (e.g., Assistant College Football Coaches).
2.  **Phase 2**: Expand to Actors (SAG-AFTRA integration).
3.  **Phase 3**: The "Super-Agent" (Cross-vertical negotiation).
