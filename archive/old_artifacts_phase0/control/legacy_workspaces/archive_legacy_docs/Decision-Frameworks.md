### PRE-MORTEM
- Define success metrics and launch scope.
- Imagine failure 3 months post-launch; list plausible causes.
- Cluster risks; rank by likelihood × impact.
- For top risks: mitigation, owner, trigger, checkpoint.

### 5-Whys
- State the problem clearly with evidence.
- Ask why repeatedly until reaching a systemic root cause.
- Capture contributing factors and missing controls.
- Define corrective and preventive actions with owners/dates.
