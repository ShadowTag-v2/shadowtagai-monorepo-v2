Short-Term Rental Property Management Agreement – Outline

1. Parties
	•	Owner: [Your Name / LLC]
	•	Manager: [Local PM Name]

⸻

2. Scope of Services

Manager shall:
	•	Handle guest communication (inquiries, booking confirmations, check-in/out instructions, reviews).
	•	Coordinate cleaning and turnovers (using local contractors).
	•	Stock basic consumables (toilet paper, soap, paper towels, trash bags).
	•	Handle maintenance coordination (owner approval required for repairs >$250).
	•	Provide emergency guest support (24/7 text/phone availability).
	•	Manage listing platforms (Airbnb, VRBO, etc.).
	•	Collect and remit local occupancy tax (if required).

⸻

3. Compensation
	•	Management Fee (Tiered):
	•	15% of gross booking revenue (before cleaning fee).
	•	If annual gross exceeds $120,000, fee increases to 18% of gross revenue above that threshold.
	•	Cleaning Fees: Paid directly by guests, passed through to cleaners. Manager may add 10–15% coordination markup.
	•	Consumables Restocking: Included in management fee.
	•	Extra Services (optional, hourly): $25/hr for owner-requested tasks outside standard duties.

⸻

4. Owner Responsibilities
	•	Maintain homeowner’s insurance and STR permit/license.
	•	Cover utilities (water, sewer, trash, electric, internet, propane).
	•	Pay for major repairs and capital improvements.
	•	Provide initial furnishings and supplies.

⸻

5. Term & Termination
	•	Initial Term: 12 months.
	•	Automatic month-to-month renewal unless 30 days’ notice given.
	•	Termination for cause (fraud, negligence, failure to perform): immediate.

⸻

6. Guest Policies (to be enforced by Manager)
	•	No smoking indoors.
	•	No pets without prior approval.
	•	Quiet hours: 10pm–8am.
	•	House + Wagon + Garage add-on rules (garage only available as add-on or with compound booking).
	•	Hot tub use at guests’ own risk, posted rules required.

⸻

7. Insurance & Liability
	•	Manager is not liable for guest-caused damages.
	•	Owner to maintain $1M+ STR liability coverage.
	•	Security deposits or damage waivers handled via platform.

⸻

8. Reporting
	•	Monthly statement of gross income, expenses, and owner net payout.
	•	Annual income summary for tax reporting.
