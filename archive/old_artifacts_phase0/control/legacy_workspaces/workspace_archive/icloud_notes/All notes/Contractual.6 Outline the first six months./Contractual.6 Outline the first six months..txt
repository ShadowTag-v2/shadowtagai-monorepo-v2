## **Location Strategy: San Francisco Optimal Neighborhoods**

### **Recommended Location: SOMA (South of Market)**

**Why SOMA is perfect for Contractual:**

- AI companies are “leading the charge for bigger space” with most activity in small office segment for seed-stage companies
- Tenant requirements jumped to 6.3M SF in Q1 2024, approaching pre-pandemic levels
- Close to legal tech ecosystem and potential customers
- Subleases in prime areas range from $60 to $80 per square foot

**Specific Target Areas:**

1. **SOMA/South Beach** - Near legal district, startup ecosystem
1. **Mission Bay** - Tech corridor, reasonable rents
1. **Financial District** - Legal community proximity

- **Budget:** $8,000-12,000/month (including utilities)
