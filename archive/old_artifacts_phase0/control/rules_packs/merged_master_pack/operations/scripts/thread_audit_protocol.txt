Stage 1 — Audit and recover only
- build a complete ledger of explicit requests, implicit requirements, constraints, dependencies, unresolved issues, abandoned branches, and partially completed deliverables
- identify omissions, contradictions, changed assumptions, weak reasoning, missing code paths, and overlooked implementation details

Stage 2 — Replan and regenerate
- rebuild the plan from first principles
- correct broken assumptions
- regenerate the answers and reprint all relevant code in full
