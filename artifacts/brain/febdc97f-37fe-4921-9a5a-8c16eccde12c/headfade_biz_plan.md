# HeadFade: Strategy & Execution
**The Gamified Turing Test Casino**

## 1. The Core Economic Engine
HeadFade is a rapid-fire, psychologically addictive Turing Test. Users swipe through bizarre dashcam footage and hyper-realistic synthetic media, voting `[REAL]` or `[AI]`. Deception is the product, and we achieve the legal immunity of a casino because the user explicitly opts into the game.

*   **B2C Revenue (The Cash Flow)**: The viral loop brings users in for free. Monetization occurs immediately via a $4.99/mo "God Mode" subscription (which unlocks Gemini's raw *Thinking* forensics detailing *why* a video is fake) and programmatic interstitial ads placed between swipes.
*   **B2B Revenue (The Moat)**: Every single vote, hesitation, and failure is recorded. The data is anonymized via PipelineDP and structured in BigQuery to generate the **Human Deception Index (HDI)**. This is a definitive mathematical dataset mapping exactly what visual artifacts fool human perception. We sell annual API access to this dataset to Enterprise Trust & Safety teams (Meta, Alphabet, DoD) via Apigee for $150k+ contracts.

## 2. Timelines & The Path to Customer #1
Because the product avoids raw GPU cluster rentals and relies 100% on Google Cloud Serverless (Cloud Run, Vertex AI API, Chrome 146 Edge AI), the fixed execution cost is exactly **$0.00**.
*   **Engineering Phase**: Complete (72 Hour Sprint). The PWA and FastAPI backend are scaffolded natively on GCP.
*   **Launch Mechanics**: Drop the application on r/LocalLLaMA, r/Damnthatsinteresting, and X with 100 hand-curated "mind-bending" seed videos. The CTA is an aggressive challenge: *"Prove you aren't blind."*
*   **Time to First Customer**: **Under 7 Days**. The first organic users hit the platform within minutes of the launch post. The first paying B2C 'God Mode' subscriber converts within 48 to 72 hours of viral traction.

## 3. Funding & Capital Structure
HeadFade is structurally designed to be a self-sustaining cash machine.
*   **Venture Capital**: **N/A**. We intentionally bypass traditional pre-seed rounds for HeadFade.
*   **Capital Velocity**: The Google Cloud Free Tier absorbs the initial viral traffic spike. The $4.99/mo subscriptions cash-flow the founder immediately. HeadFade requires zero external capital to reach profitability.
