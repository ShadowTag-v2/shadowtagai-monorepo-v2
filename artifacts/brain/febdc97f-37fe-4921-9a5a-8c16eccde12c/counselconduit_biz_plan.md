# CounselConduit: Business Plan
**The Privileged Wrapper**

## 1. The Core Economic Engine
CounselConduit is the "Shopify for Legal AI." We operate as the secure, privilege-preserving routing tier between the law firm and the core foundational models (Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, Perplexity).
*   **Revenue Model**: B2B SaaS Subscriptions. Law firms pay a tiered, auto-reloading monthly fee (e.g., Base tier starts at $299/mo).
*   **Margin Strategy**: The tiers operate like a prepaid utility (e.g., $100 API blocks). We capture an aggressive 85%+ pure software margin over raw LLM token costs.
*   **The Attorney Upside**: The lawyer uses the platform to generate client work product rapidly. They bill their client directly for the augmented legal service through their existing retainer structure. We never insert our brand between the lawyer and the client.

## 2. Timelines & The Path to Customer #1
By adopting a **Zero-Trust BYOK Strategy**, we bypass the 6-to-9 month enterprise SOC2 compliance death spiral. We do not store sensitive RAG case files long-term on our servers; we securely orchestrate API requests on the lawyer's encrypted sandbox under the protection of *United States v. Heppner (S.D.N.Y., Feb. 10, 2026)*.
*   **Month 1 (Engineering Core)**: Fork the active HeadFade monolithic stack (Next.js/FastAPI/Cloud Run). Implement the LiteLLM multi-model router, the Ephemeral Token architecture, and Stripe Connect auto-billing webhooks.
*   **Month 2 (Beta Onboarding)**: Hand-select 5-10 friendly boutique law firms. Offer whitelist access.
*   **Time to First Paid Customer**: **45 Days** from day zero codebase initialization.
