# Judge 6 / Risk OS - Commercial Strategy & SKU Catalog

## 1. Product Vision
A "Doctrine-as-a-Service" compliance engine that fuses Army ATP 5-19 (physical) + DoD CSRMC (cyber) into a unified risk assessment layer.
**Core Value:** Prevention > Detection. "Risk that used to be invisible becomes visible... and then it disappears."

## 2. 19-Layer Defense Grid (The Stack)
1. **Core Cyber + UEBA**: Google SecOps, Chronicle.
2. **Self-Harm/Suicide Prevention**: Vertex AI Safety, Crisis Redirect.
3. **Deepfake Detection**: Vertex AI Vision/Video.
4. **SynthID Verification**: Content provenance.
5. **CA Minor Protection (SB 243)**: Age inference, disclosures.
6. **EU AI Act Compliance**: Prohibited practices, transparency.
7. **Business Judgment**: Monte Carlo sims (BigQuery/Dataproc).
8. **Hacker/Phishing Mitigation**: Workspace protection.
9. **Supply Chain + Physical Risk**: ATP 5-19, Maps API, IoT.
10. **KYB/KYE Vetting**: Continuous monitoring (APIs).
11. **Harassment/Conduct**: NLP on comms.
12. **Security+ Framework**: Gap assessment.
13. **VPN Tunneling Detection**: Cloud IDS, deep packet inspection.
14. **Zero Trust/Geo-Blocking**: BeyondCorp, Access Context Manager.
15. **Cybertruck Protocol**: Telemetry/IoT grounding.
16. **Infrastructure (Antigravity)**: Serverless sovereignty.
17. **Moderation (Estop)**: Model armor.
18. **Economics/Governance Fusion**: The unified dashboard.
