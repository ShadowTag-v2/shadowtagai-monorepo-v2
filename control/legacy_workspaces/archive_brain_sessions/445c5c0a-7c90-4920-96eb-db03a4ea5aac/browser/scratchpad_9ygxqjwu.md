# Unusual Machines Website Observations

## Design
- Modern, industrial, and technology-focused.
- Uses high-quality, relevant imagery (drone components) with a dark, professional overlay.
- Clean and minimalist navigation.

## Colors
- **Main Background:** Dark navy/black (hero section).
- **Text:** White for high contrast.
- **Accents:** Blue (logo and image elements).
- **CTA Button:** Transparent with white border ("CONTACT SALES").

## Layout
- **Header:** Sticky top bar.
  - Left: "UNUSUAL MACHINES" logo.
  - Right: Navigation links (About Us, Media & Events, Investors, Contact, Careers) and accessibility controls.
- **Hero Section:**
  - Full-width background image with dark overlay.
  - Large, bold left-aligned headline: "HERE TO SERVE THE AMERICAN DRONE INDUSTRY."
  - Right-aligned stock market widget for NYSE: UMAC.
- **Accessibility:** Prominent accessibility options in the header.
