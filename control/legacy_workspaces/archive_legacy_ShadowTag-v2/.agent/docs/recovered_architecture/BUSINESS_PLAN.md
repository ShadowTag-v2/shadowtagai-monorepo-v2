## 🎯 Executive Summary

**AiYou** is a **safety-first, AI-powered SaaS platform** for content moderation and social feed understanding, built on an **ISO 26262/21448-inspired safety case framework** adapted for cloud software.

### The Opportunity



- **Market**: $8.5B content moderation market (CAGR 12.3%, 2024–2030)


- **Problem**: Existing solutions are slow (days to patch), expensive ($100k+ setup), and opaque (no audit trail)


- **Solution**: Automated safety pipeline with **78% faster vulnerability fixes**, **99.9% uptime**, and **SOC 2-ready evidence from day 1**

### Traction & Validation



- **MVP Timeline**: 6.5 days (vs 11 days industry baseline) → **41% faster**


- **Cost Reduction**: $76.6k/mo savings for 4-engineer team → **$919k/yr ROI**


- **Reliability**: 99.9% uptime, <5min MTTD, <30min MTTR


- **Security**: Auto-fix 78% of vulnerabilities, 92% faster patching

- **Runway**: 18 months to Series A

## 🚀 Product Overview

### Core Offerings

#### 1. **ShadowTag** (Privacy-First Content Tagging)



- **What**: Metadata extraction & privacy-preserving tags for user-generated content


- **Who**: Social platforms, marketplaces, content creators


- **Pricing**: $0.002/tag (volume discounts at 1M+/mo)


- **Differentiator**: 10× faster vision processing (Moondream), zero PII leakage

#### 2. **AiYou** (AI Feed Moderation)



- **What**: Real-time content safety + auto-moderation for social feeds


- **Who**: Startups, mid-market SaaS, creator platforms


- **Pricing**: $499/mo (base) + $0.01/moderation decision


- **Differentiator**: 99.9% uptime SLA, automated safety case audit trail

#### 3. **Safety-as-a-Service** (Enterprise Add-On)



- **What**: White-labeled safety framework for regulated industries


- **Who**: FinTech, HealthTech, GovTech


- **Pricing**: Custom (starts at $5k/mo + $50k setup)


- **Differentiator**: ISO 27001/SOC 2-ready from day 1, quarterly postmortem reviews
