# Valuation Standards & Methodology

## Reference: Valuing a Business (5th Edition), Pratt & Niculita

This document establishes the **Official Valuation Doctrine** for ShadowTagAI Inc. (AiYou), adopting the standards set forth by Shannon P. Pratt. All financial projections, governance audits (Judge #6), and valuation algorithms must adhere to these principles.

### 1. Fundamental Approaches

We utilize the three generally accepted approaches to value:

#### A. Income Approach


* **Discounted Future Economic Income Method (Chapter 9)**:

    * Primary method for the 2030 Risk-Adjusted Valuation ($201B).

    * **Core Formula**: Value = PV(Projected Economic Income) / (1 + Discount Rate)^n.

    * **Key Inputs**:

        * numerator: Expected Prospective Economic Income.

        * denominator: Discount Rate (Risk-Free Rate + Equity Risk Premium + Size Premium + Specific Risk).

* **Capitalized Economic Income Method (Chapter 10)**:

    * Used for stable, long-term "terminal value" estimations of specific service layers (e.g., Commerce Mall).

    * Distinction: Discounting (multi-period) vs. Capitalization (single period/constant growth).

#### B. Market Approach


* **Guideline Publicly Traded Company Method (Chapter 11)**:

    * Benchmarking against verified "Faang" distinctives.

    * *Application*: Comparing "CineVerse" metrics to Netflix/YouTube trading multiples (Revenue/EBITDA).

* **Guideline Merged and Acquired Company Method (Chapter 12)**:

    * *Application*: Analyzing acquisitions of edge-compute or AI-infrastructure firms to justify "Satellite Mesh" valuations.

#### C. Asset-Based Approach


* **Asset Accumulation Method (Chapter 14)**:

    * Revaluing all assets (tangible and intangible) to market value.

    * *Critical for ShadowTag*: Valuation of the "Intangible" Cryptographic Provenance layer and "Verified Creator" network (distinct from book value).

* **Capitalized Excess Earnings Method (Chapter 13)**:

    * Hybrid method used to isolate the value of Intangible Assets (Goodwill/Brand).

### 2. Discounts & Premiums (The "Judge" Logic)

Judge #6 algorithms must apply these rigorously:


* **Control Premium (Chapter 15)**: Added value for controlling interest (relevant to Governance tokens).

* **Discount for Lack of Control (DLOC) (Chapter 16)**: Applied to minority stakes.

* **Discount for Lack of Marketability (DLOM) (Chapter 17)**:

    * *Crucial*: Pre-IPO shares or "locked" ecosystem tokens must carry a DLOM.

    * *Mitigation*: ShadowTag verification increases liquidity confidence, potentially lowering DLOM.
