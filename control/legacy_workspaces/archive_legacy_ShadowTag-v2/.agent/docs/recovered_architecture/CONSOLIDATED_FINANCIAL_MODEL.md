The **AiYou financial model** demonstrates:

1. **Strong unit economics** across all verticals

2. **Capital-efficient growth** with 18-24 month payback periods

3. **High gross margins** (65-70%) driven by verification moat

4. **Diversified revenue** across infrastructure, content, gaming, and commerce

5. **Clear path to profitability** by 2027

6. **Significant exit potential** ($10B+ by 2030)

The combination of **defensible infrastructure**, **verified content delivery**, and **ecosystem lock-in** creates a business with both high growth and strong margins — a rare combination that justifies premium valuation multiples.

**Investment Thesis**: AiYou is building the verified AI layer that FAANG cannot replicate, with infrastructure, content, and commerce moats that compound over time.
