# LETTER OF INTENT (LOI)

**STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL**

**Date:** December 9, 2025

**To:** [Partner Entity Name - e.g., Southern California Edison / Pacific Oil Rig Co.]
**From:** AiYou Holdings / Pinkln Defense Systems
**Subject:** Strategic Partnership for High-Performance Edge Compute Deployment ("Project SkyNode")

Dear [Partner Name],

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the preliminary understanding between AiYou Holdings ("AiYou") and [Partner Entity] ("Partner") regarding the deployment of AiYou's proprietary **SkyNode** compute orchestration software ("Golden Image") onto [Partner]'s physical infrastructure facilities ("[Site]").

**1. Strategic Objective**
To transform [Site] into a sovereign, high-performance cognitive infrastructure node, leveraging [Partner]'s existing power and real estate assets combined with AiYou's asset-light AI cloud platform.

**2. Proposed Structure: "The Airbnb for Compute"**


* **AiYou Contribution:**


    * Proprietary "Golden Image" OS and Orchestration Layer.


    * Global demand routing (commercial & defense workloads).


    * Automated maintenance and "Zero-Touch" operation.


* **Partner Contribution:**


    * Site access and security.


    * Power and cooling infrastructure.


    * Network backhaul access.

**3. Economic Model**


* **Revenue Share:** Net Revenue generated by the node shall be split **50/50** between AiYou and Partner.


* **CapEx:** AiYou shall bear the cost of specialized compute hardware (if applicable). Partner retains ownership of facility infrastructure.


* **OpEx:** Power costs shall be deducted from Gross Revenue before the split.

**4. National Security Priority**
This partnership supports critical national defense initiatives. The deployed infrastructure will optionally support:


* **Tactical Edge Compute Fabric (TECF)** for DoD applications.


* **JADC2 Data Fabric** interoperability.


* **FOCI Mitigation:** Operations handled by AiYou's cleared personnel/proxies.

**5. Non-Binding Nature**
This LOI represents an expression of intent and is not legally binding, except for the Confidentiality and Exclusivity provisions.

**6. Next Steps**


* Site survey within 14 days.


* Pilot deployment of 1 rack (approx. 10kW) within 30 days.


* Definitive Agreement within 60 days.
