## 1. Purpose

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the preliminary understanding between Pnkln Corp ("Integrator") and [TOWER OWNER] ("Host") regarding the colocation of a High-Performance Compute (HPC) Edge Pod at the specified telecommunications facility.

## 2. Project Overview

Pnkln Corp intends to deploy a compact, self-contained edge compute module ("The Pod") to support low-latency satellite and terrestrial AI workloads.

**Specifications**:

- **Footprint**: 24" x 40" (approx. 4U Ground Cabinet or Rack Space).

- **Power**: 1.2 kW continuous load (110V/220V AC).

- **Connectivity**: 10Gbps handoff to on-site Backhaul or Starlink Terminal.
