# The Universal Agency ("CAA for Everyone")

**Tagline**: "Everyone deserves a shark in their corner."

## Overview

A platform that provides elite, AI-driven representation to professionals who are underserved by traditional human agencies. We democratize access to high-end negotiation, deal scouting, and career management.

## Target Verticals

1. **Actors/Creatives**: Contract negotiation, casting submission.
2. **College Coaches**: Job market analysis, buyout negotiation.
3. **Private Equity Managers**: LP matchmaking, deal flow filtering.

## Tech Stack

- **Negotiation Engine (`contract_judge.py`)**:
  - Powered by **LawJudge** (Judge #6).
  - Analyzes contracts against union standards (SAG-AFTRA) and market comps.
- **Frontend**: Next.js (Planned).
- **Infrastructure**: GKE Sovereign Cloud.

## Roadmap

1. **Prototype**: Contract upload & "Redline" analysis.
2. **Alpha**: "Assistant Coach" niche launch.
3. **Beta**: Private Equity "Capital Agent" module.
