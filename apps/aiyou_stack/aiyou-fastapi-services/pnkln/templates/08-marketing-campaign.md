Create comprehensive multi-channel marketing campaigns with research-backed messaging.

```
Create comprehensive marketing campaign:

**Campaign Overview**:
- Campaign Name: "AgentFlow Public Launch"
- Campaign Objective: Product launch + lead generation
- Campaign Timeline: 2025-12-01 to 2025-12-31 (4 weeks)
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Know Your Audience**: Research where they actually hang out
2. **Be Channel-Native**: Don't copy-paste same message everywhere
3. **Plan Ahead**: Create calendar before campaign starts
4. **Test Everything**: A/B test key messages
5. **Engage Authentically**: Especially on Twitter and Product Hunt
