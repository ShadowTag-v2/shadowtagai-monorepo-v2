# Template 01: Deep Research & Analysis

## Purpose
Conduct comprehensive research on a topic with assumption-challenging and multi-perspective analysis.

## When to Use
- Exploring new markets or technologies
- Validating product ideas
- Understanding competitive landscapes
- Making strategic decisions

---

## Copy-Paste Prompt

```
@research [YOUR TOPIC]

Conduct deep research on [YOUR TOPIC] with the following requirements:

**Research Depth**: Exhaustive
**Perspectives Required**:
- Technical/Engineering perspective
- Business/Market perspective
- User/Customer perspective
- Ethical/Risk perspective

**Key Questions to Answer**:
1. [Question 1]
2. [Question 2]
3. [Question 3]

**Challenge These Assumptions**:
- [Assumption 1 to validate/challenge]
- [Assumption 2 to validate/challenge]

**Deliverables Needed**:
- Executive summary (3-5 sentences)
- Key insights ranked by impact
- Evidence base with sources
- Challenged assumptions with alternatives
- Contradictions found in the research
- Research gaps identified
- Actionable recommendations

**Timeline**: [30 minutes / 1 hour / etc.]
```

---

## Example Usage

```
@research AI agent orchestration frameworks for 2025

Conduct deep research on AI agent orchestration frameworks for 2025 with the following requirements:

**Research Depth**: Exhaustive
**Perspectives Required**:
- Technical/Engineering perspective
- Business/Market perspective
- User/Customer perspective
- Ethical/Risk perspective

**Key Questions to Answer**:
1. What are the leading frameworks and their adoption rates?
2. What are the key differentiators between frameworks?
3. What are the emerging trends that will define 2025-2026?

**Challenge These Assumptions**:
- Multi-agent systems are always better than single-agent
- Python-based frameworks will continue to dominate
- Open-source frameworks are more widely adopted than proprietary

**Deliverables Needed**:
- Executive summary (3-5 sentences)
- Key insights ranked by impact
- Evidence base with sources
- Challenged assumptions with alternatives
- Contradictions found in the research
- Research gaps identified
- Actionable recommendations

**Timeline**: 30 minutes
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Narrow topic scope for deeper insights
2. **List Assumptions**: Explicitly state what you want challenged
3. **Define Perspectives**: Specify which viewpoints matter most
4. **Set Timeline**: Longer time = deeper research
5. **Ask Good Questions**: Frame questions to get actionable answers
