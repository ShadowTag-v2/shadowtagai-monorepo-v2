## Strategic Assessment

### Strength Assessment: PNKLN



- **Unrivaled** in high-security, low-latency edge compute.


- **Differentiation**: We process *in space*, removing the backhaul latency for decision-making (Critical for Defense/HFT).

### Weakness / Risk



- **Capacity**: Significantly lower total bandwidth capacity vs. Starlink megaconstellation.


- **Mitigation**: Partner with Starlink for bulk transport ("Orbit" Tier), focus PNKLN links on high-value command/control.
