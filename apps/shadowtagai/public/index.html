Architecture

The S.E.U. Security Stack

The credit card login is the ultimate perimeter. We are mathematically immune to the supply-chain attacks that humiliated billion-dollar AI startups.

S

Sandbox-Bound

Proxy tokens are cryptographically bound to the client's session ID and IP. Stolen tokens are rejected from any other origin.

E

Ephemeral

API tokens minted when credit card clears. Destroyed the millisecond the client logs out. Dead session = dead key.

U

User-Billed

Every token maps to the lawyer's auto-scaling tier. Rogue actors can't bleed KovelAI — they just trigger a tier upgrade.
