## Revenue Assumptions

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|

## Sensitivity Analysis

### Downside (50% of plan)
- 50 firms by end of Year 2 (instead of 250)
- $25K MRR, $300K ARR
- Still exceeds Seed threshold
- 30-month runway on $4M

### Upside (2x plan)
- 500 firms by end of Year 2
- $280K MRR, $3.4M ARR
- Accelerates Series A to Q2 2027
- Could reach cash-flow positive without Series B

### Base Case Exit Valuation (Year 5)
| Scenario | Revenue Multiple | Valuation |
|---|---|---|
| Strategic (12x ARR) | $7.8M × 12 | **$93M** |
| Growth (20x ARR) | $7.8M × 20 | **$156M** |
| Premium (35x ARR) | $7.8M × 35 | **$273M** |
