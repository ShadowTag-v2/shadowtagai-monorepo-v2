## 1. Overview & Creative North Star

This design system is built upon the North Star of **"The Transparent Authority."** In the high-stakes world of legal compliance, the interface must feel like an immutable source of truth, yet remain fluid and hyper-modern.

We treat the UI as a single, cohesive environment of **layered glass and light**. Intentional asymmetry (right-aligned technical metadata vs left-aligned editorial headlines) creates a bespoke, engineered feel. The hierarchy is defined not by lines, but by the **physical stacking of translucent surfaces**.

- **No-Line Rule:** 1px solid borders are **strictly prohibited** for sectioning. Use background shifts.

- **Signature Textures:** Buttons/gauges use gradient `primary` → `primary_fixed_dim`.

## 3. Typography

### Font Stack
| Context | Font | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Display & Headlines | Space Grotesk | 600-700 |
| Body & Legal Text | Inter | 400-500 |
| Labels & Data | Inter | 400-500 |

### Rules
- Headlines: letter-spacing `-0.02em` for tight, authoritative look
- Data points: `font-feature-settings: "tnum"` for tabular figures
- Never use browser default fonts

---

## 4. Elevation & Depth

- **Ambient Shadows:** `0px 24px 48px rgba(0, 212, 255, 0.08)` — tinted blue glass shadow
- **Ghost Border:** `outline_variant` at 15% opacity — a "glint" not a hard edge
- **Glow States:** Status indicators get `2px` outer glow in their status color
- **Roundness:** `md` (0.375rem) and `lg` (0.5rem) — sharp and technical, not bubbly

---

## 5. Component Patterns

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `primary_container` + `on_primary_container` text
- **Secondary (Glass):** `surface_bright` at 20% opacity + ghost border
- **Tertiary:** Text-only `primary` color + underline on hover

### Cards
- No 1px dividers. Use `0.75rem` whitespace or alternating backgrounds.
- Glassy cards get `0.5px` top-edge highlight in `primary_fixed` at 20%

### Input Fields
- Background: `surface_container_lowest`
- Bottom-only 2px border in `outline_variant`
- Focus: bottom border glows `primary`

### Compliance Gauges
- Concentric rings: `primary_container` (progress) + `surface_variant` (empty)
- Central figure: `display-sm`

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use asymmetrical layouts (wide narrative left, narrow metadata right)
- Apply `backdrop-blur` to all overlays
- Use `tertiary` sparingly — only for PASS states

### ❌ Don't
- Use pure black `#000000` or pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Use high-contrast shadows
- Use Material Design rounded corners (24px+)
- Use 1px horizontal dividers between list items
