# Pomelli GTM Plan for HeadFade

## Overview
HeadFade is the Truth Layer of the internet, turning every AI video into a gamified forensics experience. This plan details the Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy derived from the Pomelli Business DNA extraction.

## Objectives
- Launch HeadFade public presence.
- Highlight the "Truth Layer" value proposition.
- Drive user engagement with the gamified Turing Test.

## Campaign Structure
- **Launch Sequence**: Staged Twitter thread (`launch-thread.md`) announcing the release.
- **B2B Outreach**: 50 targeted emails to creators and enterprise partners.
- **Continuous Marketing**: 7-day and 30-day autonomous execution sequences.

## Directory Structure
- `/campaigns/` - Contains the active and scheduled outreach templates.
- `/assets/` - Marketing images, diagrams, and video elements.
- `/emails/` - B2B cold outreach sequences.
