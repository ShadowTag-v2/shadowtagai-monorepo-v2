Privilege-First Architecture

Everything Your Firm Needs to Stay Protected

Built on the legal doctrine established in Heppner, every feature is designed to keep client research non-discoverable while accelerating your revenue.

Kovel Attestation

Cryptographic HMAC-SHA256 hash per session. Each query produces an immutable attestation receipt proving privileged communication under the Kovel doctrine.

Multi-Model Routing

Route client queries through Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, and Perplexity — all behind your firm's privileged portal. Clients choose the best model for their research.

Front-End Revenue

Clients subscribe to your AI portal with their credit card. Funds flow directly to your firm's Stripe account. Get paid on the front-end for every query.

Oracle Memo

A 7-stage AI pipeline generates comprehensive legal memorandums with citations, case law references, and structured analysis — visible only to the supervising attorney.

Dead-Man's Switch

Client's ephemeral research portal auto-wipes after inactivity. No export. No copy. No print. Screen content automatically destroyed to preserve privilege.

Judge 6 Policy Gate

Mandatory governance layer on every model route. Controls export permissions, transcript generation, and regulated-domain answers. Your firm sets the rules.
