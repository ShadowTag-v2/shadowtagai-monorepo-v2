Privacy & Security Policies

Strict Safe-Harbor, GDPR, and Heppner-Compliant Intake Isolation.

1. Zero-Egress Sandbox

Our AST execution matrices operate fundamentally on the Edge. No personal identifiable data (PID) from client intake form payloads leaves the execution boundary of the sovereign instance without explicit cryptographic authorization.

2. SOC2 Type II Assurance

We undergo strict continuous compliance audits. Real-time data streams via WebSockets (e.g., our CRM matrix) are E2E encrypted, relying solely on standard Firebase SDK streams authenticated via strict Institutional RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) policies codified natively in our Rules Matrix.

3. EU AI Act Obligations

All automated reasoning loops mapping client risk matrices output transparent AST evaluation logs available upon discovery request. We assert "no black box" metrics mathematically prior to payload completion.

4. Data Retention

Lead data is retained dynamically strictly during the active lifecycle of an intake operation. Tombstoned leads are wiped from memory cascades and disk clusters adhering to a rigid 14-day cycle protocol.
