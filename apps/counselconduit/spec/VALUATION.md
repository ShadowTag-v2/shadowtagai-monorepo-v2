## Narrative

CounselConduit is the commercial wedge. pnkln / uphillsnowball is the internal engine.
The separation lets the business story stay simple while the technical engine compounds in the background.

**Core thesis**: We are not selling legal research. We are selling emotional security under attorney-client privilege. The gap between AI cost (~$0.02/query) and perceived value ($149–$999/mo) is **emotional arbitrage** — and it produces 85–91% gross margins.

---

## Competitive Moat (4 Layers)

1. **Privilege Layer**: *Heppner* precedent — only platform with Kovel attestation receipts
2. **Emotional Layer**: S.E.U. framework — competitors do research, we do reassurance
3. **Switching Cost**: Attorney workflows + client data locked behind privilege wall
4. **Network Effect**: More attorneys → more client trust → more referrals
