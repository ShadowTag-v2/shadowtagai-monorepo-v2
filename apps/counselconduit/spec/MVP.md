## Wedge
Stateless legal SaaS workflow with premium pricing, Kovel attestation, and S.E.U. emotional architecture.

## Core Thesis
> Nobody is buying "AI attorney research". They are paying for **the feeling that someone smart has their back**.

## Product Principles
1. **Emotional first, legal second** — S.E.U. framework (Safety → Empathy → Utility)
2. **Google-native runtime** — Cloud Run, Firestore, Cloud Tasks
3. **Premium SaaS economics** — 85-91% gross margin
4. **Invisible engagement** — No timers, no "are you still there", ambient micro-animations
5. **Privilege-preserving** — Kovel attestation receipts (HMAC-SHA256)
6. **High-trust output** — Oracle Studio 7-stage pipeline + Judge 6 policy gate
