### [Welcome to Google Earth Engine](https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/guides/getstarted)

[Google Earth Engine](https://earthengine.google.com/) is a geospatial processing service. With Earth Engine, you can perform geospatial processing at scale, powered by [Google Cloud Platform](https://cloud.google.com/). The purpose of Earth Engine is to:

* Provide an interactive platform for geospatial algorithm development at scale
* Enable high-impact, data-driven science
* Make substantive progress on global challenges that involve large geospatial datasets
