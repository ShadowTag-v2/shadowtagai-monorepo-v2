## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Detailed About Product**: More context = better research and positioning
2. **Know Your Customer**: Specific ICP > vague "developers"
3. **Share Constraints**: Budget, timeline, team size all matter
4. **Define Success**: What does "successful launch" mean?
5. **Trust the Process**: Let ProjectDeepAgent orchestrate the workflow

---

## What Happens Behind the Scenes

The ProjectDeepAgent orchestrates:

1. **ResearchAgent** investigates market, competitors, positioning
2. **RevenueAgent** designs pricing based on research findings
3. **CopyAgent** + **DesignAgent** execute in parallel on landing page
4. **CopyAgent** creates email sequences aligned with messaging
5. **WorkflowRefiner** builds launch workflow and automation

All outputs are synthesized into integrated launch package.
