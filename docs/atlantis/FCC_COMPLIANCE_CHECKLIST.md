# FCC Compliance & Site Safety Checklist (Tower-Edge Pilot)

## 1. NEPA (National Environmental Policy Act) Review


- [ ] **Wilderness Areas**: Is the site located in an officially designated wilderness area?

- [ ] **Wildlife Preserves**: Is the site in a wildlife preserve?

- [ ] **Endangered Species**: Will deployment affect listed threatened or endangered species?

- [ ] **Historic Districts**: Is the site on the National Register of Historic Places?

- [ ] **Tribal Lands**: Does the site affect Indian religious sites?

- [ ] **Flood Plain**: Is the facility located in a flood plain?

- [ ] **High Intensity Light**: Will the facility use high intensity white lights in residential neighborhoods?

## 2. RF Exposure (OET Bulletin 65)

The Edge Pod includes wireless telemetry and backhaul interfaces.

- [ ] **MPE Calculation**: Perform Maximum Permissible Exposure check.

    - *Note: Edge Pod emissions are typically < 1 Watt, well below thresholds, but must be documented.*

- [ ] **Signage**: Verify appropriate RF warning signage is present at the site if standard limits are approached.

- [ ] **Worker Safety**: Ensure installation crew is aware of active transmitting antennas on the tower.

## 3. Interference & Spectrum


- [ ] **Part 15 Compliance**: Verify all compute hardware (GPUs, Servers) are FCC Part 15 Class A (Industrial) or Class B (Residential) compliant.

- [ ] **Non-Interference**: Equipment must not cause harmful interference to existing tower tenants (Cellular/Microwave).

- [ ] **Shielding**: Ensure enclosure provides adequate EMI shielding.

## 4. FAA (Federal Aviation Administration)


- [ ] **Tower Height**: (N/A for ground cabinets). If adding antennas > 20ft above structure, Form 7460-1 may be required.

- [ ] **Lighting**: Verify existing tower lighting is operational and not obstructed by new equipment.

## 5. Construction & Local Zoning


- [ ] **Structural Analysis**: (If tower-mounted) Confirm tower loading capacity with Structural Engineer.

- [ ] **Permits**: Obtain local electrical/building permits for ground cabinet installation.
