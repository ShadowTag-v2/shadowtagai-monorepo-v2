# Disciplined Entrepreneurship (MIT Framework)

## 24-Step Approach to Building a Successful Startup

This framework is adopted as the **Strategic Execution Doctrine** for ShadowTagAI Inc., guiding product development and market entry for CineVerse, GamePort, and Commerce Mall.

### Theme 1: Who is Your Customer?


1. **Market Segmentation**: Brainstorm a wide variety of potential customers and markets for your idea.

2. **Select a Beachhead Market**: Choose **one** market segment to focus on and win over first. This is the entry point.

3. **Build an End User Profile**: Create a detailed profile of the ideal customer within the beachhead market.

4. **Calculate the Total Addressable Market (TAM) Size for the Beachhead Market**: Estimate the total revenue you could generate if you captured 100% of your beachhead market.

5. **Profile the Persona for the Beachhead Market**: Go beyond the user profile and create a detailed story about one specific, real person who represents the ideal customer.

### Theme 2: What Can You Do For Your Customer?


6. **Full Life Cycle Use Case**: Map out how your persona will use your product, from the moment they become aware of it to the end of its life.

7. **High-Level Product Specification**: Create a visual representation and description of your product.

8. **Quantify the Value Proposition**: Clearly state the tangible benefits your product will provide to the customer.

9. **Identify Your Next 10 Customers**: Find at least 10 potential customers who are similar to your persona.

10. **Define Your Core**: Determine the **one thing** you do better than anyone else that is difficult to copy. ( *ShadowTag Context: This is the Cryptographic Provenance/Verification Layer* )

11. **Chart Your Competitive Position**: Visually represent where your product stands in relation to competitors based on the persona's top two priorities.
