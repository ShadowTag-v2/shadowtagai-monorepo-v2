## Part 7: Risk Analysis & Mitigations (Compliance Framework Framework)

### RA-1 (Critical Risks)

**Risk 1.1: Gemini API Reliability**

- **Threat**: Google outage breaks entire Pinkln stack
- **Impact**: 100% downtime, customer churn, SLA violations
- **Mitigation**:
  1. Multi-model fallback (Gemini → Claude → GPT-4)
  2. Local PyTorch backup for critical kernels
  3. 99.9% uptime contractual guarantee from Google
  4. Circuit breaker pattern (auto-failover in <500ms)
- **Residual Risk**: RA-3 (monitoring mitigates)

**Risk 1.2: DTE Evolution Degrades Performance**

- **Threat**: Self-evolution introduces regressions
- **Impact**: Accuracy drops, customer trust lost
- **Mitigation**:
  1. Canary deployment (test evolved prompts on 5% traffic)
  2. A/B testing with statistical significance (p<0.05)
  3. Auto-rollback if accuracy drops >1%
  4. Human-in-the-loop approval for RA-1 decisions
- **Residual Risk**: RA-4 (extensive testing mitigates)

---

### RA-2 (High Risks)

**Risk 2.1: Regulatory Compliance Failure**

- **Threat**: ShadowTag audit trails fail SOC 2 / FedRAMP audit
- **Impact**: Cannot sell to enterprise/government
- **Mitigation**:
  1. Pre-audit with certified firm (Drata, Vanta)
  2. Penetration testing (quarterly)
  3. Cryptographic proofs (Ed25519, Merkle trees)
  4. Immutable GCS logs (write-once, read-many)
- **Residual Risk**: RA-3 (third-party validation mitigates)

**Risk 2.2: Competitive Response**

- **Threat**: AutoGen/LangGraph copies Gemini function calling approach
- **Impact**: Lose differentiation, price pressure
- **Mitigation**:
  1. DTE self-evolution moat (hard to replicate)
  2. Glicko-2 ratings (unique IP)
  3. ShadowTag integration (defensible with crypto)
  4. Speed to market (6-12 month lead time advantage)
- **Residual Risk**: RA-2 (continuous innovation required)

---

### RA-3 (Medium Risks)

**Risk 3.1: Customer Concentration**

- **Threat**: Top 5 customers = 60%+ of revenue
- **Impact**: Single churn event craters MRR
- **Mitigation**:
  1. Diversify across industries (defense, healthcare, finance)
  2. Annual contracts with early renewal incentives
  3. Quarterly business reviews (QBR) to ensure ROI
  4. Net Promoter Score (NPS) monitoring (target >50)
- **Residual Risk**: RA-3 (acceptable for early-stage)

### RA-4 (Low Risks)

- **Mitigation**:

- **Residual Risk**: RA-4 (expected in early-stage)
