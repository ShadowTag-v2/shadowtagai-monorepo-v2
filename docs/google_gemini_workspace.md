# Google Gemini Deep Research with Workspace Data

Google announced **Gemini Deep Research**, a new capability that merges data from Workspace apps (Gmail, Drive, Chat) with web content to provide richer, context‑aware results. The feature is currently available on desktop, with mobile access slated for later.

## Highlights

- **Workspace integration**: Pulls in emails, documents, and chat history to enhance search and generation.
- **Desktop first**: Live preview and Slack integration are in development.
- **Related announcements**:
  - _Nano Banana Pro_ on Flow for Ultra subscribers – high‑quality image generation.
  - _Warp Agents 3.0_ – full terminal use.
  - _Antigravity AI IDE_ – free public preview of Google’s AI‑powered development environment.
