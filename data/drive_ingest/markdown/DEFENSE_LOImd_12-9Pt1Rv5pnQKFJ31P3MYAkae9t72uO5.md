Dear [Partner Name],

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the preliminary understanding between ShadowTag-v2 Holdings ("ShadowTag-v2") and [Partner Entity] ("Partner") regarding the deployment of ShadowTag-v2's proprietary **SkyNode** compute orchestration software ("Golden Image") onto [Partner]'s physical infrastructure facilities ("[Site]").

**1. Strategic Objective**
To transform [Site] into a sovereign, high-performance cognitive infrastructure node, leveraging [Partner]'s existing power and real estate assets combined with ShadowTag-v2's asset-light AI cloud platform.

**2. Proposed Structure: "The Airbnb for Compute"**

- **ShadowTag-v2 Contribution:**
  - Proprietary "Golden Image" OS and Orchestration Layer.

  - Global demand routing (commercial & defense workloads).

  - Automated maintenance and "Zero-Touch" operation.

- **Partner Contribution:**
  - Site access and security.

  - Power and cooling infrastructure.

  - Network backhaul access.

**3. Economic Model**

- **Revenue Share:** Net Revenue generated by the node shall be split **50/50** between ShadowTag-v2 and Partner.

- **CapEx:** ShadowTag-v2 shall bear the cost of specialized compute hardware (if applicable). Partner retains ownership of facility infrastructure.

- **OpEx:** Power costs shall be deducted from Gross Revenue before the split.

**4. National Security Priority**
This partnership supports critical national defense initiatives. The deployed infrastructure will optionally support:

- **Tactical Edge Compute Fabric (TECF)** for DoD applications.

- **JADC2 Data Fabric** interoperability.

- **FOCI Mitigation:** Operations handled by ShadowTag-v2's cleared personnel/proxies.

**5. Non-Binding Nature**
This LOI represents an expression of intent and is not legally binding, except for the Confidentiality and Exclusivity provisions.

**6. Next Steps**

- Site survey within 14 days.

- Pilot deployment of 1 rack (approx. 10kW) within 30 days.

- Definitive Agreement within 60 days.
