# Pricing doctrine — v8.3

## Core rule
Price from verified value created, then anchor packaging to locked business context.
Use: direct expense savings, opportunity value unlocked, compliance and risk reduction, workflow acceleration that is actually measured.
Do not use: hype, raw TAM theater, valuation language unsupported by product proof.

## Locked business anchors
These anchors are fixed unless explicitly amended:
- **Consumer Syndicate**: $149/mo (95% margin)
- **Enterprise Base SLA**: $20,000/mo (69-71% margin)
- **Enterprise EU26 Premium**: $28,333/mo (76-78% margin)
- **Sovereign Scale**: 100% compute pass-through.

## Hive / edge economic doctrine
- Consumer economics assume centralized intelligence and cheap stateless edge execution.
- Enterprise economics assume isolation, stronger controls, and sidecar costs.
- Do not price enterprise like consumer micro-edge software.
